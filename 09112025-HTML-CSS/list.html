Mythical Creatures

dragon
A dragon is a mythological, often serpent- or lizard-like creature appearing in world folklore, known in some cultures as a benevolent, wise symbol, and in others as an evil, fire-breathing beast.
phoenix
"Phoenix" can refer to a legendary bird that is reborn from its own ashes, symbolizing immortality and resurrection, or it can refer to the capital city of Arizona, a major city in the United States known for its agriculture and rapid growth.
vampire
A vampire is a mythical, legendary, or fictional creature that sustains itself by feeding on the vital essence, typically blood, of the living.
werewolf
A werewolf is a legendary creature, also called a lycanthrope, that is a human who can shapeshift into a wolf or a hybrid wolf-human form. This transformation is often involuntary, occurring under the influence of a full moon, and is a prominent theme in folklore and modern media like films and books.
